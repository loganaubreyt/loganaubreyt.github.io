Eric's Late Night Delivery
White rice, double chicken, fajita veggies, lettuce, fresh salsa, corn salsa, guacamole
A classic DoorDash companion. Order when Chipotle is about to close and they are running low on some items. A bit expensive with double meat and added guacamole, and no beans is a minus in my book.

Joyce's Literally-entirely-cheese Bowl
Brown rice, black beans, chicken, cheese, lettuce, fresh salsa, queso
For the cheese lovers! Smother away your worries in this basic bowl. Not a lot of variety, but the queso helps balance out the blandness. Personally too creamy of a mouthfeel for me, your experiences may vary.
